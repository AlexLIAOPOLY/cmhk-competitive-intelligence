     Income Tax Law”) and the “Detailed Rules for the Implementation of the Enterprise Income Tax Law of the People’s Republic
     of China” (the “Implementation Rules”); and (iii) information obtained from the SAT, the Company is required to withhold and
     pay enterprise income tax when it pays the 2022 Interim Dividend to its non-resident enterprise shareholders. The enterprise
     income tax is 10% on the amount of dividend paid to non-resident enterprise shareholders (the “Enterprise Income Tax”), and the
     withholding and payment obligation lies with the Company.
